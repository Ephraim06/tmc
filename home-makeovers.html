With our years of experience, we can not only give you expert advice but we can also save you money.

Re-spraying and modernizing of existing kitchens

TMC Construction specializes in the re-spraying of existing wooden or melamine kitchens (It is not recommended to re-spray Foil wrapped kitchens). We make use of industrial catalyzed hardwearing paints that offer a durable tough and long lasting finish. This also allows our customers to choose from an extremely wide variety of colours and finishes, whilst keeping costs affordable.

The process involves removing doors for sanding and preparation and the masking and preparation of onsite carcasses. This is a 2 step process also allowing customers to make use of their kitchen whilst doors are being prepared.

Design, manufacture and installation of new kitchens

TMC Construction has many years experience in the design of kitchens and keeps abreast with modern trends. In close consultation with our clients, we design and produce 3D visual layouts, once agreed and accepted, TMC will manufacture the entire kitchen at our factory before installing on site. Our fitters are diligent and tidy at all times ensuring the least possible inconvenience to customers.

Renovation and modernization of bathrooms

Many of Cape Town's finest homes find themselves with outdated tiling and sanitaryware in their bathrooms. Our company, in consultation with clients, design and make recommendations of the latest trends in bathrooms. We source the highest quality tiles, baths, shower screens and fittings. Our team will move on to site and gut an existing bathroom in a short space of time before professionally remodelling to our customers wishes.

Painting of residential premises both inside and out

We have a professional team of painters on hand and offer an affordable painting service. Care is always taken and our clients premises are kept clean and tidy through out the process.

Roof cleaning

Our professional cleaning team is equipped with industrial grade high pressure cleaners and related products ensuring that roofs are quickly but professionally cleaned. TMC carries insurance in the event of any unforeseen accidents or breakages.

General Household repairs and small construction projects:

TMC has a dedicated and experienced building team, whilst we don’t undertake major construction projects we do have many years experience in small general construction projects and repairs such as removing interior and exterior walls, opening up kitchens and lounges, remodelling bedrooms, kitchens, outside entertainment areas, tiling, screeding of floors, repairs to retaining walls and moving, closing or opening windows and doorways.

TMC Construction is a local small business that takes pride in its service delivery, we have a work team that has grown with the company who are trustworthy and experienced in their respective fields. We offer a professional but affordable service and most importantly come with TRACEABLE REFERENCES.
